What's The Goal?

Brave Hond aims to improve the quality of life for dogs, dog owners, and the general public.

Remove The Poop!

Uncleaned dog poop is a significant issue that affects dog owners and the general public alike. We all want it to be better.

Improve Relations Between Dog Owners And The General Public

For many people dogs are foreign and sometimes scary. We'd like to build a social network to facilitate understanding and empathy on both sides.

Foster Relationships Amoung Dog Owners

For dog owners it's very helpful to have the support of your fellow dog owners. We want to make it easier for dog owners to make friends and help each other out.

Reduce Trash In Non-Pedestrian Areas

As dog owners will tell you, legal dog spaces in the city are often overloaded with trash. Our goal is to use the community to improve that for everyone.

How Will It Work?

Brave Hond is a multifaceted approach to these problems.

A Social Network For Dogs And Their Owners
  • Allow dog owners to self-organise, and help each other out with dog sitting & walking, or community clean ups.
  • Strengthen the sense of community amoung dog owners which will encourage individual and group responsibility.
  • Allow the public to learn about dogs in their area, and communicate with their owners.
Convenient Educational Resources
  • Educate current and potential dog owners on the responsibilities and expectations of dog ownership.
  • Educate the public on how to interact with dogs and their owners.
Community Governance & Reporting Mechanism
  • Elected community leaders who can act as moderators and advocates for their neighbourhood.
  • A user friendly way for dog owners to monitor and report community issues such as problem areas or problem animals.
  • Realtime tracking and reporting of the community's needs and progress.
On The Ground Initatives
  • Free poop bags supplied at dog parks.
  • Signage to educate the public on the program and drive users towards the website & app.
  • The end of "geen opruimplicht"!
